A bit about me...

I’ve always been known as a jack of all trades but had no clue what the ‘one’ I wanted to master would be.

In high school, I discovered a love for furniture design because of the sense of satisfaction I felt from working on a piece I thought up myself. I enjoyed how elegant yet powerful a craft it was. From here, I knew I wanted a future where I could exercise my sensibilities and logic, and at the same time, let my sense of creativity shine through.

So I entered the Bachelor of Design Computing – now a UX designer in training, looking to graduate by the end of 2021.

Right now, my key strengths lie in UI design and Front-end development - they also happen to be my FAVOURITE areas to work in! Motion design always fascinates me, and my passion for furniture has lived on through my work in 3D modelling.

My time in the UX world so far has been challenging, granted, but knowing that I save people from subpar experiences, inspires me to be here for the long haul.
